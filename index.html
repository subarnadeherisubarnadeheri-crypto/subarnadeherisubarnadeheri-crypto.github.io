Aaj Ka Rashifal 20 February 2026: In Chaar Rashi Walo Ko Rehna Hoga Satark
By Admin | Updated 20 Feb 2026
Aaj 20 February 2026 ka din kai rashiyon ke liye mahatvapurn rahega. Grahon ki sthiti mil-juli prabhav de rahi hai. Janiye sabhi 12 rashiyon ka vistaar se rashifal.
⚠️ Vishesh Suchna: Aaj Mesh, Kark, Vrishchik aur Makar rashi walon ko arthik aur karyakshetra se jude faisle soch samajh kar lene chahiye.

♈ Mesh Rashi

Aaj aapko kaam ke maamle me satark rehna hoga. Office me vivaad ki sthiti ban sakti hai. Arthik len-den me saavdhani rakhein.

♉ Vrishabh Rashi

Aaj dhan laabh ke yog ban rahe hain. Ruke hue kaam poore ho sakte hain. Parivaar ka sahyog milega.

♊ Mithun Rashi

Thoda tanav mehsoos ho sakta hai. Kisi purane dost se baat hogi jo man halka karegi.

♋ Kark Rashi

Sehat ka vishesh dhyan rakhein. Anavashyak kharch se bachen. Parivaarik maamle me shanti banaye rakhein.

♌ Singh Rashi

Career me naye avsar mil sakte hain. Aapka atmavishwas badhega.

♍ Kanya Rashi

Nivesh ke liye samay anukool hai. Mehnat ka phal milega.

♎ Tula Rashi

Faisle lene me jaldbazi na karein. Partnership me laabh ho sakta hai.

♏ Vrishchik Rashi

Karyakshetra me satark rehne ki zarurat hai. Gupt shatru sakriya ho sakte hain.

♐ Dhanu Rashi

Yatra ke yog ban rahe hain. Vidyarthiyon ke liye din shubh hai.

♑ Makar Rashi

Kaam ka pressure badh sakta hai. Arthik mamle me saavdhani rakhein.

♒ Kumbh Rashi

Business me laabh ke sanket hain. Nayi zimmedari mil sakti hai.

♓ Meen Rashi

Dharmik karyon me man lagega. Khushkhabri mil sakti hai.
